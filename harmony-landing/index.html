More about Algo Trading

• An RL(Reinforcement Learning) based agent that can trade in defined scenarios using real-time data

• A low-risk agent that tries to understand the ups and downs of the market and trades rationally

• The trained model at this time, can achieve 4-5% above the market’s natural profit without the trading fees (1-2% by considering the trading fees)

• A built-in stop-loss mechanism

• Still needs a supervisor for a good starting point

• Acts better in the up-trend markets

Adding Grid Bot to Wallex

Grid Trading Bot is a trading bot that assists you in carrying out the Grid Trading Strategy. It enables you to place a series of purchase and sell orders within a given price range. When a sell order is fully executed, the bot instantly places another purchase order at a lower grid level, and vice versa. This robot guarantees a definite profit

Crypto Payment Gateway

The crypto payment gateway enables merchants to use Bitcoin and altcoins for their business and also to accept payments in traditional currencies such as USD, Euro, etc. It also provides plugins for eCommerce platforms.
